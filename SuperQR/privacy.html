Privacy Policy.

Overview

SuperQR (the "App"), developed by the The Dhakar Developers team, is built to respect your privacy. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Package Name: com.dharmdada.superqr.app

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. All scanning, generation, and history data stays strictly on your device. We do not use external servers to process your QR codes, and we do not track your location or behavior.

Device Permissions

To provide its functionality, SuperQR requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to scan QR codes and barcodes. Visual data is processed locally and never uploaded.
  • Storage: Used to save generated QR codes to your gallery or load existing images for scanning.
  • Flashlight: Used to assist scanning in low-light environments.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google ML Kit for on-device barcode recognition. Google may collect diagnostic information to improve their libraries, but no content from your scans is transmitted.
